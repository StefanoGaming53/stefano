Brave the wasteland in this post apocalyptic top-down shooter. Unlock a variety of weapons through raids against adaptive enemies that act independently. Your top priority is survival, no matter what it takes. 
Find out who the real monsters are... the infected, or the people left to survive?

Imagine a game like Hotline Miami, but if it was an extraction shooter similar to Escape from Tarkov, with a little extra Silent Hill vibes. What you get is dark corridors, intense gunfights, terrifying enemies, and a LOT of blood.

Controls:
- Movement - W,A,S,D
- Shoot/Attack - Lmb
- Reload - R 
- Swap weapons - 1,2,3,0
- Use Medkit - Q
- Throw Grenade - G
- Toggle Flashlight - F
